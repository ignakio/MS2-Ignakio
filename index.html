About

A sailing voyage needs some planning even for an experienced sailor. Voyager is a checklist for Sailors to pack & plan before they set off on an overnight voyage. We want you to sail more and worry less.

We have been part of planning voyages for sailors from departure points including Cape Town, Sydney and Dublin. We look forward to being part of your voyage.
